Meet the Visionaries

Our Featured Designers.

David Tlale

South African fashion icon

Known for his bold colors, luxurious fabrics, and innovative silhouettes, Tlale has taken the fashion world by storm.

Gert-Johan Coetzee

"Couturier to the Stars"!

Coetzee's designs are a captivating blend of luxury and innovation. He's not afraid to push the envelope, incorporating cutting-edge techniques like 3D printing into his collections.

Gavin Rajah

Effortless elegance

Beyond his design talent, Rajah is deeply passionate about empowering others. He founded The White Light Movement, a non-profit organization that supports women and girls affected by abuse.

Thebe Magugu

Redefining Menswear and Identity.

Magugu isn't just a designer; he's a storyteller who uses fashion to explore themes of social justice, history, and personal identity.
