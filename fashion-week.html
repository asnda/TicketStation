About The Event

About The Event

South African Fashion Week advocates for local designers amidst a trend of uninspired imports by retailers. The call is for mainstream sectors, including international chains, to embrace local production, fostering brand growth. Government support is urged to revive the textile industry, offering incentives for modernization. Africa, particularly South Africa, stands poised to lead with sustainable slow fashion, attracting global attention. It's time to rally behind our designers, recognizing fashion's pervasive influence and potential for positive change.

Where Fashion Takes Center Stage

Step into the heart of South African fashion! Fashion Week takes place at iconic venues across the country, transforming them into vibrant hubs of creativity. Witness established designers showcase their latest collections alongside rising stars pushing the boundaries of style. From historic landmarks to contemporary art spaces, each venue offers a unique backdrop for the latest trends. Don't miss this chance to experience the electric atmosphere and be among the first to see what's shaping the future of South African fashion.
